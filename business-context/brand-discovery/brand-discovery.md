## Brand Voice

- Direct, no-fluff
- Confident but not arrogant
- Local and human — not corporate
- Rugged and grounded — Northwoods work ethic
- Speaks to homeowners like a trusted neighbor who vets contractors for them

**Core tension to own:** "It takes forever to get started."
Every headline, CTA, and ad should implicitly or explicitly promise speed-to-contact.

**Voice examples:**
- "Stop guessing who to call. We connect you with the right builder fast."
- "Local deck contractors, ready to quote."
- "One form. Multiple quotes. Zero hassle."
- "Most homeowners wait weeks just to hear back. Not here."
- "Ready to build? So are they."

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

| Role | Color Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary Accent | Crafted Orange | `#e54c00` |
| Dark Base | Timber Slate | `#323434` |
| Secondary Text | Builders Grey | `#646760` |
| Sky Accent | Wisconsin Sky Blue | `#83b2cf` |

**Rationale:** Milwaukee Tool-inspired. High contrast, trade-credible, pops on mobile ads. Crafted Orange signals urgency and action. Timber Slate grounds it as professional. Wisconsin Sky Blue adds outdoor depth.

### Typography (recommended system)

- **Headlines:** Bold condensed sans-serif (e.g., Barlow Condensed Bold)
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter, DM Sans)
- **Labels/tags:** All-caps, tracked, small size

### Logo Concept

- **Mark:** Wisconsin state silhouette — simplified, geometric, filled in accent orange or charcoal
- **Wordmark:** "CENTRAL WISCONSIN" in condensed bold all-caps, "DECK BUILDERS" on second line
- **Lockup options needed:**
  - Horizontal (mark left, wordmark right)
  - Stacked (mark above wordmark)
  - Icon-only (favicon, social profiles)

### Visual Style Rules

- High contrast always — dark backgrounds with orange accents, or white with charcoal/orange
- Bold, heavy typography — no thin or delicate fonts
- Rugged & Northwoods aesthetic: cedar, pine, natural wood, lake-country settings
- **Photography split by ad goal:**
  - Trust-building ads → craftsmanship: wood grain, framing, tools, precision work
  - Conversion ads → lifestyle: families on decks, summer evenings, grills, outdoor gatherings
- No generic stock photos — Wisconsin settings, authentic regional feel
