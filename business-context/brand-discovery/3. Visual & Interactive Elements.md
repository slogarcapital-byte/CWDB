### 2.5. Project Examples/Gallery

*   **Headline:** `Inspiring Decks Built in Central Wisconsin` (Charcoal, bold).
*   **Visual:** Grid of high-quality images showcasing various deck styles and sizes. Mix of craftsmanship (close-ups of details) and lifestyle (finished decks).
*   **Call to Action (Secondary):** `See More Projects` (If applicable, links to a gallery page).

### 2.6. Call to Action (Repeat)

*   **Headline:** `Ready for Your Dream Deck?` (Charcoal, bold).
*   **Sub-headline:** `Get connected with Central Wisconsin's best deck builders today.`
*   **CTA Button:** `GET MY FREE QUOTE` (Burnt orange background, white text, bold, prominent).

### 2.7. Footer

*   **Logo:** CWDB icon-only or small horizontal lockup.
*   **Navigation:** Links to Privacy Policy, Terms of Service.
*   **Contact:** Small text for general inquiries (e.g., `info@cwdeckbuilders.com`).
*   **Copyright:** `© 2026 Central Wisconsin Deck Builders. All rights reserved.`

## 3. Visual & Interactive Elements

*   **Typography:** Adhere to the brand style guide (Barlow Condensed for headlines, Inter/DM Sans for body).
*   **Color Palette:** Use charcoal, burnt orange, off-white, and medium gray as per the brand guide.
*   **Forms:** Embed Tally form for quote requests. Ensure form fields are clear, concise, and easy to complete on mobile.
*   **Responsiveness:** Design must be fully responsive for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
*   **Speed:** Optimize images and code for fast loading times, crucial for conversion.
