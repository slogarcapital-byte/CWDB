## Primary Color Palette

Our color palette is built for high contrast and to evoke rugged professionalism with an energetic accent. Colors are inspired by Wisconsin timber, outdoor hardware, and natural sky.

| Role | Color Name | Hex Code | RGB Value | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary Accent** | Crafted Orange | `#e54c00` | R229 G76 B0 | CTAs, highlights, key visual elements. Signals warmth, energy, and action. |
| **Dark Base** | Timber Slate | `#323434` | R50 G52 B52 | Backgrounds, primary text, dominant elements. Conveys professionalism and depth. |
| **Secondary Text** | Builders Grey | `#646760` | R100 G103 B96 | Subheadings, secondary information, borders, subtle UI elements. |
| **Sky Accent** | Wisconsin Sky Blue | `#83b2cf` | R131 G178 B207 | Complementary secondary accent. Evokes the outdoors and natural light. |
| **Wood Accent** | Aged Cedar | `#8B5A2B` | R139 G90 B43 | Warm wood accent. Process step connectors, testimonial card left borders, city card bottom borders, cedar divider strip. Use sparingly. |

---

## Usage Guidelines

*   **High Contrast:** Always prioritize high contrast. Use Timber Slate on white, or Crafted Orange/white on Timber Slate backgrounds.
*   **Accent sparingly:** Crafted Orange is a powerful accent — use it strategically for CTAs and key information, not as a dominant background color.
*   **Sky Blue as complement:** Wisconsin Sky Blue softens layouts and adds outdoor depth. Use for secondary accents, icons, focus rings, and service card borders — not primary CTAs.
*   **Aged Cedar sparingly:** Use only for warm wood-themed accents — left borders on testimonial cards, bottom borders on city cards, process step connectors, and the cedar divider strip between hero and content. Never use as a large background fill.
*   **Cedar tint:** `rgba(139,90,43,0.15)` — approved for `.section--cedar` block backgrounds and testimonial location badges.
*   **Consistency:** Maintain consistent use of these colors across all brand touchpoints — website, advertising, email, and print.
*   **Accessibility:** Ensure sufficient contrast ratios for all text and interactive elements.
