## Ad Copy Strategy

Facebook/Instagram ads will focus on both **lifestyle** (inspiring homeowners with the dream of a new deck) and **problem/solution** (addressing the frustration of finding reliable contractors). Visuals will be key, featuring high-quality images of completed decks in Central Wisconsin settings or families enjoying their outdoor spaces.

### Ad Set 1: Lifestyle & Inspiration

**Headline:** Transform Your Backyard into an Oasis!
**Primary Text:** Dreaming of a new deck for summer gatherings? Central Wisconsin Deck Builders connects you with trusted local pros for fast, hassle-free quotes. Enjoy outdoor living sooner!
**Call to Action:** Get Free Quote
**Visual Idea:** High-quality image of a beautiful, spacious deck with people enjoying it (e.g., family BBQ, sunset view).

### Ad Set 2: Problem/Solution (Speed & Reliability)

**Headline:** Tired of Waiting? Get Your Deck Quote FAST!
**Primary Text:** Don't waste weeks searching for a reliable deck builder. Central Wisconsin Deck Builders connects you with vetted local contractors who respond quickly. "Fast Quotes. Trusted Builders."
**Call to Action:** Get Instant Quote
**Visual Idea:** Image of a homeowner looking frustrated, or a split image showing a clock/calendar next to a happy homeowner on a new deck.

### Ad Set 3: Local Expertise & Trust

**Headline:** Local Central WI Deck Pros, Ready to Build!
**Primary Text:** Support local and get the deck you deserve. We partner with the best deck builders in [[Wausau]], Stevens Point, and surrounding areas. Quality craftsmanship, guaranteed. 
**Call to Action:** Find a Builder
**Visual Idea:** Image showcasing craftsmanship (e.g., close-up of quality wood, a builder working) or a map pin over Central Wisconsin.

### Ad Set 4: Direct & Urgent

**Headline:** Deck Project? Get 3 Free Quotes Now!
**Primary Text:** Ready to build? Fill out one simple form and get connected with multiple trusted deck contractors in Central Wisconsin. Stop the endless search, start building today.
**Call to Action:** Get My Quotes
**Visual Idea:** Clean, high-contrast image of a modern deck, or a graphic highlighting "3 Free Quotes."
