## 3. Attribution Diagnosis (the centerpiece; Jim's hypothesis confirmed)

The chain breaks in three consecutive places, none of them the ad platforms:

**Ads side is healthy.** Google auto-tagging ON plus tracking template appends full UTMs; every ad click lands with gclid + UTMs intact. Google's own gtag immediately persists gclid in the `_gcl_aw` cookie. Google Search fundamentals: 6,248 impressions, 420 clicks, ~6% CTR, ~$3 CPC, only 19.9% impression share (budget-limited in peak season).

**BREAK A (primary): no attribution persistence on the site.** All quote CTAs are bare `/get-a-quote` links; there is no localStorage/sessionStorage keeper and the relay reads only `location.search` at submit time. Any visitor who browses before submitting (gallery, FAQ, then back to the form) submits with zero attribution. Only same-pageview submitters survive; that is why only Reist (1 of 13) carried a gclid. Evidence: `website/scripts/hubspot_form_relay-1.0.0.js`.

**BREAK B: relay never sends `hutk`.** The HubSpot tracking cookie exists on every visit and an empty hidden `hutk` field literally sits in the form; the relay ignores both, so HubSpot's own original-source join is degraded too. (HubSpot's native `hs_analytics_source` still caught the truth via its page-tracking script: Johnson, Quinn, Reist = PAID_SEARCH; Petersen = PAID_SOCIAL.)

**BREAK C: conversion-tag layer is a graveyard.** Google Ads has 13 conversion actions; the one primary (`from_submit_quotes`) went silent 6/10 (that is the "conversion tracking error" nag Jim sees), the GA4-import actions that counted ~25-30 conversions were deleted, four GBP "Local actions" junk conversions are marked primary, TWO different AW accounts (18113251301 + 10862517194) fire site-wide, and two GA4 properties double-track everything. Meta's Pixel has NEVER fired a Lead event; the OUTCOME_LEADS campaign has optimized blind since April.

**BREAK D (warehouse): even captured attribution goes nowhere.** The pull reads only custom utm/gclid contact properties (never `hs_analytics_source`), and `attributed_campaign_id_via_gclid` is never populated (no click-level ingestion).

**Minimal fix (~2-4 hours total):** (1) 25-line site-wide attribution-keeper script (first-touch params to localStorage, 90-day expiry); (2) relay v1.1: param-or-storage fallback, extend key list (utm_medium/term/content, fbclid), add hutk to context; (3) consolidate Google Ads to ONE primary lead conversion, demote GBP junk to secondary, remove the stale AW-10862517194 tag; (4) fire Meta Pixel Lead on submit; (5) warehouse maps hs_analytics_source as attribution fallback.
