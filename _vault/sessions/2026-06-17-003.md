## Decisions Made
- **Structure (Jim + partners):** SBG shared-services / captive-labor group, NOT a merger. Phase A co-op now; Phase B merger in 1 to 2 years on rules set now.
- **Entities:** three SBG sibling entities owned 1/3 each by the individuals, funded by equal cash; SBG-Labor taxed as an S-corp; SBG-RealEstate formed but dormant.
- **Pay:** partners draw an $80/hr W-2 market wage; SBG bills the LLCs at market and reinvests; derived billable rate ~$145/hr WITH WC (~$135 elect-out).
- **Lead engine** stays 100% CWDB's and is deliberately downplayed in all partner-facing materials (Jim's negotiating strategy).
- **Old $1,000-per-accepted-bid lead-purchase deal** with Ben and John is being wound down.
- **CEO verdict:** the partnership is viable and the SBG structure is more protective for Jim; but do NOT quit the $140k W-2 cold in June on 3 months reserve. Bank a season or confirm Ashley's income covers the winter trough.
