## Decisions Made

- **New Build = `full_tearout` matrix with `demo: "N"` + $0 demo rate** (net-new, full footings, zero tear-out cost). **Fence = separate LF-based calculator**, not a deck variant.
- **TimberTech: treat John's numbers as material COST/LF**, convert to $/SF (x2.18) and mark up like Trex (~x2.75). Markup + board-coverage are the two tunable constants. Provisional until John confirms.
- **Color can change price:** each color may carry a `sell_per_sf`/`sell_per_lf` override; defaults inherit the line price.
- **Mock-ups: fully automatic at send-time, transform the customer's actual yard photo, 2 renders (wide + detail), Gemini 2.5 Flash Image.** PDF emails to Jim's inbox first, so he is the de facto review gate.
- **Excel made data-driven** (shared `PDB`/`PB` range maps) rather than re-anchored with fixed rows, so future material additions never break formulas.
- **AI-rendering legal mitigations (all implemented):** (b) stronger "COMPUTER-GENERATED SIMULATION" disclaimer; (c) scope-subordination clause in "What's Not Included" when renderings present; (d) burned-in "AI SIMULATION - ILLUSTRATION ONLY" watermark on every render; (e) privacy note requiring a PAID Gemini tier with no-training terms.
