## Decisions Made

2. **Quote structure: itemized fixed price.** Line items roll to single total. Best mix of homeowner transparency and operator margin protection.

5. **Final quote on Nayak: $4,900.** Itemized as $1,400 prep / $750 materials / $2,300 application (includes 14 stairs and full railing system) / $450 site protection. Per-deck-sq-ft of $13.84, top of premium tier.

7. **Card payment added to accepted methods.** Per client insistence. Final wording: "cash, check, credit or debit card, or digital payment (details provided upon acceptance)."
8. **Em dashes permanently banned from all writing for Jim.** Standing rule. Documents, emails, code comments, chat, generated content. All of it. Replace with period, colon, comma, parens, or "and"/"for". Saved as `feedback-no-em-dashes.md`. Reason: AI-tell, not authentic.
9. **Card processor: QBO Payments primary, Venmo Business backup.** Per cwdb-ceo-operator recommendation. ACH at 1% capped $10 wins on cost; native QBO sync wins on bookkeeping; B2B invoicing capability wins on contractor-side use case for the $1,000 accepted-bid model. Live target before 2026-05-12.

12. **No "Insured" claim on the document.** Insurance for Jim performing the actual work is unresolved. Misrepresenting on a binding doc is the kind of detail that goes from minor to ruinous if anything goes wrong on site.
13. **Email hosting blocker surfaced.** cwdeckbuilders.com has no MX records. info@ is inbound-only via GoDaddy forwarding. Sending FROM info@ requires real email hosting (Google Workspace or GoDaddy M365). Decision deferred to Jim, recommendation = Workspace.
