# Phase 4 — Marketplace Expansion

**Goal:** Expand into multiple trades. Multiple contractors receive each lead.

## New Verticals
- Roofing
- Bathroom remodels
- Concrete
- Basement finishing
- Pole barns

## Revenue Model Evolution
Multiple contractors bid on each lead → increases revenue per lead.

## Long-Term Vision
Regional contractor lead marketplace. Comparable to Angi, Thumbtack.
Potential revenue: $50K–$150K per month across multiple states.

## Prerequisites
- [[Phase 3 - Replication]] proven across multiple cities
- Geographic expansion stable and profitable
