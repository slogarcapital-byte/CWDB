# Phase 2 — Profitability

**Goal:** Build a stable lead generation funnel.

## Targets
- 20–50 leads per month
- Revenue: $3K–$10K per month
- Focus: Optimize cost per lead and conversion rates

## Prerequisites
- [[Phase 1 - Validation]] must be complete
- At least one contractor actively buying leads
- Ad campaigns running and generating data

## Key Metrics
- Cost per lead: <$60
- Revenue per accepted bid: $1,000
- Cost per accepted bid: <$400
- ROI: 2x+ advertising return
