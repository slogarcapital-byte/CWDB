# Decision: Single Landing Page → 21-Page Authority Site

## Context
Original Phase 1 plan called for a single landing page. Realized that a full authority site with city pages, blog, and cost calculator would dramatically improve local SEO and conversion.

## Decision
Expand from single landing page to **21-page authority site** including:
- Homepage, About, FAQ, Gallery, Our Builders
- 5 city-specific landing pages ([[Wausau]], [[Schofield]], [[Weston]], [[Mosinee]], [[Merrill]])
- Cost calculator
- Blog with 4 SEO articles
- Get a Quote + Thank You
- Privacy + Terms

## Impact
- Full website plan: `/business-context/website-plan.md`
- Significantly more work but much stronger SEO foundation
- City pages target "[city] deck builders" searches
- Blog drives organic traffic over time
- Cost calculator is a lead magnet
