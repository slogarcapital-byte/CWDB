# Decision: Brand Name — Central Wisconsin Deck Builders

## Context
Business needed a public-facing brand identity before building the website.

## Decision
- **Brand name:** Central Wisconsin Deck Builders (CWDB internally)
- **Domain:** cwdeckbuilders.com (registered on GoDaddy)
- **Tagline:** "Fast Quotes. Trusted Builders."
- **Colors:** Crafted Orange (#e54c00), Timber Slate (#323434), Builders Grey (#646760), Wisconsin Sky Blue (#83b2cf)

## Impact
- All customer-facing materials use "Central Wisconsin Deck Builders"
- "CWDB" used internally only (favicons, small UI, agent references)
- Domain registered and pointed to [[Webflow]]
- Full brand guide created at `/business-context/brand-discovery/`
