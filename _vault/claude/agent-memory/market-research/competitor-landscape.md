# Competitor Landscape

**Why:** Understanding competitors informs positioning, pricing, and differentiation.

**How to apply:** Use when crafting ad copy, sales pitches, and market expansion decisions.

## National Competitors
| Platform | Model | Threat Level |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Angi (formerly Angie's List) | Lead marketplace | Medium — strong brand, high fees |
| Thumbtack | Pro marketplace | Medium — contractor-facing |
| HomeAdvisor | Lead gen | Medium — merged with Angi |
| Houzz | Directory + marketplace | Low — design-focused |

## CWDB Differentiation
- **Local focus** — we know Central Wisconsin specifically
- **Pay per accepted bid** ($1K) vs. pay-per-lead — contractors only pay when they win
- **[[Nextdoor]] community presence** — builds trust locally
- **Lower contractor cost** — national platforms charge $15-75/lead regardless of outcome

## Local Competitive Landscape
- Fragmented — most deck builders have basic websites or just a Facebook page
- No dominant local lead gen platform in Central Wisconsin
- Contractors rely on word-of-mouth, yard signs, and occasional Google Ads
- **Opportunity:** first-mover advantage in structured local lead gen
