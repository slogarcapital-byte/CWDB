=====================================

- Home & Garden
- Home Improvement & DIY
- Real Estate
- Outdoor / Lifestyle

Behavior:
- Engaged with home improvement content
- In-market for home services

=====================================
NOTES
=====================================
TikTok geo-targeting is less precise than Google/Meta.
Target Wisconsin state-level and filter by zip code in the landing page form.
Use form data to measure actual geographic quality of TikTok leads.
Compare cost per qualified lead vs. Google and Facebook monthly.

TikTok is an awareness and top-of-funnel channel — not primary lead gen.
Budget allocation: Start at 10–15% of total ad spend.
