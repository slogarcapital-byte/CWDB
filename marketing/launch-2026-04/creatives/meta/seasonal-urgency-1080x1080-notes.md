## Photo asset

## Creative rationale
- Template B (photo-full with overlay) with a deliberate seasonal eyebrow treatment that gives the creative a timestamp feeling without manufacturing urgency tropes (no red countdowns, no "LIMITED TIME").
- Eyebrow uses a small tick (`::before` 28px × 2px white bar) as a quiet visual anchor — pattern worth extracting for future seasonal creatives.
- Orange CTA is the only orange. Eyebrow is white/78% opacity (did NOT tint orange — that would break the one-appearance rule).
- `hero-merrill.webp` has the best "lifestyle deck ready" energy in the available photo set.

## Self-critique notes
- Initial draft had eyebrow in orange and CTA in white → fixed both. Orange now lies once, on CTA.
- Photo-bottom is where furniture sits — scrim ramp is positioned so the sofa/pillows are still visible but darkened enough for text contrast.
- "SUMMER SLOTS OPEN" in trust row doubles as a soft secondary urgency push without competing with the CTA.
