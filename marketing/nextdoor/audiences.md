MARKET: Central Wisconsin — Deck Building Leads

## TARGETING OVERVIEW

Nextdoor targeting is neighborhood-level — more granular than Facebook or Google.
Primary filter: Homeowners only (Nextdoor verifies home addresses).

## PAID AD AUDIENCE SETTINGS

**Location:** Select all 6 zip codes above (or select by neighborhood name within each city)
**Homeowner status:** Homeowners only — Nextdoor verifies this via address
**Age:** No restriction needed — Nextdoor audience is naturally skewed toward homeowners (typically 30–65)
**Household type:** Single-family homes preferred (if filter available)

**Budget guidance:**
- Start: $5–$10/day per campaign ($100–$200/month test)
- Primary lead gen channel alongside Google Ads — not awareness-only
- Scale spend when CPL is confirmed <$60

## WHY NEXTDOOR IS PRIORITY

Market research confirms Nextdoor is the primary platform where [[Central Wisconsin Deck Builders LLC|Central Wisconsin]] homeowners ask for contractor recommendations. Key advantages vs other platforms:

- **Lower ad competition:** Fewer advertisers than Google or Facebook = lower CPL
- **Pre-verified homeowners:** Nextdoor verifies home addresses — no renters, no spam
- **Neighborhood trust:** Users trust recommendations from neighbors more than search ads
- **High intent:** People asking "who should I hire?" are actively looking to hire
- **Organic opportunity:** Free lead gen via post monitoring is unique to this platform

## TRACKING & UTM PARAMETERS

Always append UTM parameters to landing page links:

Paid ads: `?utm_source=nextdoor&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=deck-central-wi`
Organic replies: `?utm_source=nextdoor&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=deck-central-wi`

This allows the Analytics Agent to correctly attribute leads by channel in reports.
