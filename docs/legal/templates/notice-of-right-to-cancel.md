## PLAIN-ENGLISH HEADER: WHEN THIS NOTICE IS LEGALLY REQUIRED

**Read this before you decide whether to attach this Notice.**

This Notice gives a homeowner the right to cancel a home improvement contract within three business days. It exists because of two laws that work together:

- The **federal Cooling-Off Rule**, 16 CFR Part 429 (the FTC rule on door-to-door and off-premises sales), and
- The **Wisconsin counterpart** in Wis. Admin. Code ATCP 110 (Home Improvement Practices) and the home-solicitation cancellation provisions of Wisconsin consumer protection law under Wis. Stat. ch. 100.

**You MUST give this Notice (two completed copies, handed to the buyer when they sign) when ALL of the following are true:**

1. The sale is a consumer transaction with a homeowner for personal, family, or household purposes (a residential deck build or stain qualifies), AND
2. The contract is solicited or signed at the buyer's home, or at any place that is not CWDB's regular place of business (for example, signed at the homeowner's kitchen table, at a home show booth, or anywhere other than the CWDB office), AND
3. The total price is above the threshold amount that triggers the rule (the federal rule applies to sales of $25 or more made at the buyer's residence, and $130 or more at other temporary locations; nearly every deck project clears this threshold).

**When you do NOT strictly need this Notice:** If the homeowner comes to CWDB's regular place of business and signs there, the home-solicitation cooling-off right generally does not apply. Even then, attaching the Notice is harmless and a good safety practice.

**Consequence of getting this wrong:** If the Notice is required and CWDB fails to deliver it properly, the buyer's three-day clock does not start, and the buyer may be able to cancel the contract long after the work is signed (in some cases indefinitely). When in doubt, attach and deliver this Notice. Two copies must be given to the buyer at the time of signing, and CWDB must orally inform the buyer of the right to cancel.

**How to fill it out:** Enter the transaction date, the cancellation deadline (midnight of the third business day after the transaction date, not counting Sundays and federal holidays), and CWDB's address. Give the buyer both copies. Keep proof of delivery.

**YOU MAY CANCEL THIS TRANSACTION, WITHOUT ANY PENALTY OR OBLIGATION, WITHIN THREE BUSINESS DAYS FROM THE ABOVE DATE.**

If you cancel, any property traded in, any payments made by you under the contract or sale, and any negotiable instrument executed by you will be returned within TEN (10) BUSINESS DAYS following receipt by the Seller of your cancellation notice, and any security interest arising out of the transaction will be cancelled.

If you cancel, you must make available to the Seller at your residence, in substantially as good condition as when received, any goods delivered to you under this contract or sale; or you may, if you wish, comply with the instructions of the Seller regarding the return shipment of the goods at the Seller's expense and risk.

If you do make the goods available to the Seller and the Seller does not pick them up within twenty (20) days of the date of your Notice of Cancellation, you may retain or dispose of the goods without any further obligation. If you fail to make the goods available to the Seller, or if you agree to return the goods to the Seller and fail to do so, then you remain liable for performance of all obligations under the contract.

Wausau, WI 54401
