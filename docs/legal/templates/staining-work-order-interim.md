## PLAIN-ENGLISH SUMMARY (not part of the signed agreement)

**What this is.** A short, signable home improvement contract for **staining, sealing, or refinishing an existing, structurally sound deck only.** It lets CWDB lawfully take a deposit and start work now. Because the work is a cosmetic surface finish on a sound deck and does not alter, repair, or build structure, it requires no building permit, so the Wisconsin DSPS Dwelling Contractor Certification gate (Wis. Stat. 101.654) does not block it.

**What this is NOT for.** Do not use this for any deck build, rebuild, structural repair, board replacement beyond cosmetic, footing or ledger work, framing, railing rebuild, or anything that pulls a permit. Those jobs use the full **Home Improvement Contract (Deck Build and Stain)** template and wait for the license and insurance gate to clear.

**ATCP 110 still applies.** Staining a deck is "home improvement" under Wis. Admin. Code ATCP 110. A written, signed contract is required when CWDB takes a deposit (ATCP 110.05(1)(a)) or solicits the sale at the home (ATCP 110.05(1)(b)). This document carries the ATCP 110.05(2) required contents and the three-day right to cancel.

**Conspicuous-type note.** When this template is rendered to a signable PDF, the sections marked **CONSPICUOUS** (the Right to Cancel block and the Notice to Owner) must be printed in bold and in a type size no smaller than, and preferably larger than, the body text.
