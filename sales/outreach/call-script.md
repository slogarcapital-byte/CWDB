CONTRACTOR SALES CALL SCRIPT
Use for: Cold and warm calls to deck contractors
Last Updated: [DATE]

=====================================
OPENER
=====================================
"Hi, is this [NAME]?

Great — my name is [YOUR NAME]. I'll be quick. I run a lead generation service for deck contractors in [[Central Wisconsin Deck Builders LLC|Central Wisconsin]] and I'm looking for one local contractor to work with in your area. Do you have 2 minutes?"

[If yes → continue]
[If busy → "No problem. When's a better time to call back? I have something you might like."]

=====================================
PITCH (keep under 60 seconds)
=====================================
"We generate homeowner leads — people who are actively looking to get a deck built or replaced. They fill out a quote form on our website, we verify the information, and we send the lead straight to you by text and email.

Right now we're getting leads from [[Wausau]], [[Schofield]], [[Weston]], and a few surrounding cities.

We work with one contractor per territory — so whoever I partner with gets all the leads exclusively.

Are you taking on new deck projects right now?"

=====================================
IF INTERESTED — QUALIFY
=====================================
- "What's your typical project size?"
- "Do you focus on new builds, repairs, or both?"
- "What areas do you cover?"
- "How do you currently get new customers?"

=====================================
PRICING CONVERSATION
=====================================
"Here's how the pricing works — and I think you'll like it:

You don't pay for leads. You pay $1,000 only when you win the job.

So we send you the lead. You go out, give the homeowner a quote. If they hire you — you pay us $1,000. If they don't — you owe us nothing.

The average deck job is $10,000–$30,000. So you're paying us about 3–10% of the job only when you get paid. Zero risk."

=====================================
OBJECTION HANDLING
=====================================

"I don't need leads, I'm fully booked."
→ "That's great — when does your schedule open back up? We can plan to start then. And honestly, the best time to build a pipeline is when you're busy."

"How do I know the leads are any good?"
→ "Fair question. I'll send you the first lead for free. No commitment. You can judge the quality yourself before spending anything."

"I already use [Angi / HomeAdvisor / etc.]"
→ "I've heard that from a lot of contractors. The difference here is you're the only one receiving the lead — it's not shared with 3–5 other contractors. Higher close rate, less competition."

"How much does it cost?"
→ [Go to pricing conversation above]

=====================================
CLOSE
=====================================
"Let's do this — I'll send you one lead on the house so you can see exactly what you'd be getting. All I need is your email and the best number to text you. Does that work?"
