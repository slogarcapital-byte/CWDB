# Scope template - New Build (No Tear-Out)

Used by `sales/estimates/deck_calculator.py` for project type:
- `New Build (No Tear-Out)`

Brand-new deck where none existed: new footings, framing, decking, rails, and
stairs, with NO demolition / tear-out / waste-removal cost. Same variables as
`scope-build.md` (demo bullets removed).

---

## OVERVIEW
{project_phrase} at the project address. The job builds a new deck of approximately <b>{deck_sf} sq ft of deck floor</b> ({length} ft x {depth} ft) with <b>{railing_lf} linear feet</b> of railing system{stair_overview_phrase}. The new deck features <b>{decking_phrase}</b> decking, <b>{railing_phrase}</b> railings, and <b>{framing_phrase}</b> framing built to current Wisconsin residential code. There is no existing deck to remove. Work includes complete site protection, code-compliant structure, and a final homeowner walkthrough.

## SCOPE
- Site survey, layout, and homeowner walkthrough of the staked footprint before any digging
- Move and protect homeowner furniture, grill, plants, and adjacent landscaping
- Cover and protect siding, windows, doors, and any nearby paved surfaces
{footing_phrase}- Frame the new deck per WI residential code: {framing_phrase}, ledger lag-bolted and flashed to house rim, code-compliant joist spacing on 16 in centers
- Install {decking_phrase} decking boards with hidden fasteners (composite) or 3-inch stainless deck screws (wood), maintaining manufacturer-specified gap spacing
- Install matching {decking_phrase} fascia on exposed rim ({fascia_lf} LF)
- Install {railing_phrase} railing system ({railing_lf} LF) with code-compliant baluster spacing and structural post anchoring{stair_scope_line}
- Final touch-ups, complete cleanup, and removal of all construction debris
- Final homeowner walkthrough and punch-list resolution

## SCOPE_NOTE
The scope above assumes standard residential site access (driveway accessible to delivery truck), no underground utilities within the deck footprint, level or near-level grade at the build location, and existing house siding/framing in sound condition at the ledger location. Any discoveries during excavation or framing (undisclosed utilities, unstable soil, rot at the ledger location) will be discussed before continuing work, and any change order will be priced separately and added in writing.

## INCLUDED
- All materials: {framing_phrase} framing, {decking_phrase} decking, {railing_phrase} railings, hardware (joist hangers, hurricane ties, lag bolts, fasteners), code-compliant flashing
{footing_inclusion_phrase}- All labor for footings, framing, decking, railing, and stairs
- Permit and engineering allowance (homeowner is named as homeowner on permit)
- Final job-site cleanup and haul-off of all construction debris
- <b>10-year limited workmanship warranty</b> on framing and structural connections
- Manufacturer warranties pass through on decking ({decking_phrase}) and railing ({railing_phrase})

## NOT_INCLUDED
- Landscape restoration beyond the immediate deck footprint (sod replacement, planting bed restoration, irrigation repair)
- Electrical, plumbing, gas, or hot tub utility hookups (separate trade required)
- Pergolas, screens, outdoor kitchens, or other built-ins unless added to scope in writing
- Structural modifications to the house itself (door replacement, header changes, siding alterations beyond the ledger flashing detail)
- Site grading, drainage modifications, fill, or retaining wall work
- Any work required to remediate discoveries (hidden utilities, unstable soil, code violations at ledger attachment), quoted separately if found
- Painting or staining of new decking (composite does not need staining; wood decking can be quoted as an add-on for first-year finish)
- Snow removal during winter installs
