## Introduction

[[Central Wisconsin Deck Builders LLC|Central Wisconsin Deck Builders]] ("CWDB," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website cwdeckbuilders.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or submit information through our forms.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you submit a quote request form, we collect the following information:

- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Property address or city/zip code
- Deck project details (type, size, materials, timeline, budget)
- Any additional comments or project notes you provide

### Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Referring website or source
- Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
- Approximate geographic location (city/region level, derived from IP)

This information is collected through cookies, pixels, and analytics tools as described below.

---

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following analytics and advertising tools on our website:

### Google Analytics 4 (GA4)
We use GA4 to understand how visitors interact with our website — which pages are visited, how long users stay, and where traffic comes from. GA4 may use cookies to collect anonymized usage data. Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram)
We use the Meta Pixel to measure the effectiveness of our Facebook and Instagram advertising campaigns. The pixel tracks actions like page views and form submissions to help us optimize ad targeting. Meta's privacy policy: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/

### Nextdoor Pixel
We use the Nextdoor conversion pixel to measure the performance of advertising on the Nextdoor platform. Nextdoor's privacy policy: https://nextdoor.com/privacy_policy/

### Microsoft Clarity
We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how users interact with our website through session recordings and heatmaps. Clarity does not collect personal information from form fields. Microsoft's privacy policy: https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

### Cookie Management
Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of our website.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **To provide our service:** We use your project details and contact information to match you with a qualified, vetted deck contractor in your area.
- **To communicate with you:** We may contact you by email or phone to confirm your request, provide updates, or follow up on your experience.
- **To improve our website:** We use analytics data to understand how visitors use our site and to improve the user experience.
- **To measure advertising performance:** We use tracking pixels to evaluate the effectiveness of our ad campaigns and optimize our marketing spend.
- **To maintain records:** We keep records of quote requests for operational and business purposes.

---

## How We Share Your Information

### Contractor Partners
When you submit a quote request, we share your contact information and project details with the vetted contractor(s) in our network who are matched to your project. This is the core function of our service — connecting you with a qualified builder.

We share only the information necessary for the contractor to contact you and prepare a quote:
- Your name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Property location
- Project details

### Service Providers
We may share anonymized or aggregated data with service providers who help us operate our website and business, including:
- Website hosting ([[Webflow]])
- Form processing (Tally)
- Analytics (Google, Microsoft)
- Advertising platforms (Meta, Nextdoor, Google)
- Customer relationship management (HubSpot)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request, such as a court order or subpoena.

### What We Do NOT Do
- We do **not** sell your personal information to data brokers, marketing lists, or unrelated third parties.
- We do **not** share your information with contractors outside our vetted network.
- We do **not** use your information for purposes unrelated to our deck builder matching service.

---

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Specifically:

- **Quote request data** is retained for up to 24 months to support follow-up, service improvement, and business records.
- **Analytics data** is retained according to the default retention settings of each platform (e.g., GA4 retains data for 14 months by default).
- **Advertising data** is retained by the respective platforms according to their own policies.

You may request deletion of your personal information at any time (see Your Rights below).

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access:** You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correction:** You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
- **Deletion:** You may request that we delete your personal information from our records.
- **Opt-out of communications:** You may opt out of any follow-up emails or communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

**Email:** info@cwdeckbuilders.com

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

- Secure form submission via encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- Access controls limiting who can view personal data
- Use of reputable, security-conscious third-party service providers

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete it promptly.
