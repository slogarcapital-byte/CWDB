### Inputs

1. **Deck Size**
   - 10×12 (120 sq ft) — Small
   - 12×16 (192 sq ft) — Medium
   - 14×20 (280 sq ft) — Large
   - 16×24 (384 sq ft) — Extra Large
   - Custom (enter square footage)

2. **Material**
   - Pressure-Treated Wood — $15–25/sq ft
   - Cedar — $25–40/sq ft
   - Composite (Trex, TimberTech) — $30–60/sq ft
   - PVC/Vinyl — $40–70/sq ft

3. **Features** (checkboxes, add to base cost)
   - Railing (adds $20–45/linear ft of perimeter)
   - Stairs (adds $500–2,000)
   - Built-in Seating (adds $1,000–3,000)
   - Pergola or Cover (adds $3,000–8,000)
