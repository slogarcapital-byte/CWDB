## Services grid (4 cards)

### New Deck Construction
Ground-level, elevated, wraparound, and multi-level decks built for how you actually use your yard. We work in composite, PVC, cedar, and pressure-treated lumber, and we build for Wisconsin frost depth and snow load from the footings up.

### Deck Replacement
When the posts are rotting, the ledger is pulling away, or the boards are past another coat of stain, we tear out the old deck and build one that lasts another two decades. We handle the demo, the permit, and the build.

### Deck Repair
Soft boards, loose railings, wobbly stairs, failing fasteners. Not every deck needs replacing. We'll tell you straight at the walk-through whether a repair buys you real years or just delays the inevitable.

### Deck Refinishing & Staining
Got a solid deck that just looks tired? We clean, sand, and stain or reseal it so it looks new without the cost of a rebuild. This is work we self-perform and have completed for Wausau-area homeowners.
