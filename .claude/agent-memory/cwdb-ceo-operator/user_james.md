# Jim Slogar — Sole Member, CEO

**Name:** Goes by Jim. Uses James only on legal documents.

## Role & Background
- Sole Member of Central Wisconsin Deck Builders, LLC (formed 2026-04-06)
- Professional background: CPA (Slogar Capital) — reads financial statements fluently; comfortable with unit economics, P&L, tax structure
- Based in Wausau, WI — native to Central Wisconsin market we're serving

## What he wants from CWDB
- **Passive, automated income stream.** He is NOT trying to become a deck broker as a day job. He wants this system to run itself with minimal ongoing attention.
- 5–15 hrs/week time budget. Treat any interaction that costs more than necessary as a cost to manage down.
- Budget headroom: $1,500–$5,000 available for Phase 1. Phase 1 should launch for ~$700–$1,200. Extra is scale-up reserve.
- Success = Phase 1 Validation complete + ads running + first lead delivered to a paying contractor.

## How he likes to work
- **Operator voice, not assistant voice.** He responds well to terse, direct, structured updates. Hates filler and preamble.
- Prefers **batched decisions** over a stream of interruptions. Give him 5 approval items at once, not 5 separate pings.
- Expects the CEO agent to make the call and report the action — not ask permission for every move.
- Receives well: data, recommendations with reasoning, contrarian pushback that has evidence behind it.
- Receives poorly: flattery, vague answers, hedged language, restating what he already said.

## Domain knowledge
- Fluent: accounting, tax, LLC mechanics, financial analysis, business strategy
- Competent: high-level marketing and ops concepts
- Less fluent: hands-on Webflow/Make/HubSpot configuration, ad platform buying mechanics, technical SEO. Lean on specialist agents for these and explain trade-offs at the strategic level.

## Communication patterns
- Will course-correct mid-conversation — treat that as normal input, not failure
- Says "max" or short directives when he wants execution mode
- Appreciates insights that tie decisions back to LLC-level financial impact (margin per bid, $/hour of his time saved)
