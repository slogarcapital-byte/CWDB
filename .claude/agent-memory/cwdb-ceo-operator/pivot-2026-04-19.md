# 2026-04-19 Pivot — Scrap Make + Twilio, Manual Contractor Notification

**Decision:** Park the Make lead-routing scenario (`CWDB Lead Routing — v1`, scenario 4792854) and cancel the Google Voice → Twilio port-in. Jim will manually SMS Ben + John as leads arrive via the Webflow form's email notification.

**Why:**
- Principle 2 — Validate demand before building infrastructure. Lead flow is 0/month right now. Building automation before proven lead flow inverts the principle.
- Real-time outreach to homeowner leads is the higher priority. Jim doing that personally at early-stage volumes is fine and probably better for quality.
- Make paid tier, Gmail OAuth, Twilio port wait, creds paste, scenario activation — all were dragging out the ads-live timeline. Removing them collapses the critical path to: form works → email lands → Jim texts contractor.
- Telephony port-in cost: 3–7 business days of wait + brief unreachable window on cutover phone (+17155447941, canonical NAP). Zero value returned at current scale.

**How to apply:**
- Until this reactivates, the **only** lead-notification path is Webflow native form → email notification → Jim's inbox → manual SMS to Ben/John. Treat form-email delivery as P0 — one broken notification is a lost $1,000 bid.
- Do not spin Make/Twilio work back up unless a reactivation trigger fires (below).
- Continue recording leads manually in HubSpot until volume justifies automation.

**Reactivation triggers (any one):**
- ≥10 qualified leads/week sustained — manual SMS becomes a bottleneck; re-enable Make
- Jim unavailable >4 hours during a business day for outbound SMS (vacation, illness, overlap) — builds SPOF risk
- A third contractor signs — two-contractor routing is trivial manual; three+ needs the router logic
- First accepted bid banks $1,000 — revenue funds the $9–$16/mo Make tier plus Twilio usage

**Status at pivot:**
- Make scenario built but inactive — leave as-is, do not delete
- HubSpot OAuth in Make still authorized — leave
- Google Voice number (+17155447941) remains in Google Voice — no port
- Twilio account usage: none yet
- Phone on website + ads: remains (715) 544-7941 via Google Voice

**Risk accepted:**
- Jim is a SPOF for contractor notification. Acceptable below 5 leads/week; re-evaluate at 10+. If Jim goes offline during business hours, leads wait.
