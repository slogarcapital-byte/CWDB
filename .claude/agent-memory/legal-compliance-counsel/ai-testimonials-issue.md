## Issue
All testimonials displayed on the CWDB website (cwdeckbuilders.com) are AI-generated. They are not from real customers. This was confirmed by Jim on 2026-04-04.

## Risk Level: HIGH — LEGAL BLOCKER

## Legal Authority
- **FTC Act Section 5** — prohibits unfair or deceptive acts or practices
- **16 CFR Part 255** (FTC Endorsement Guides, updated June 2023) — endorsements must reflect honest opinions of actual endorsers; fabricated reviews/testimonials are per se deceptive
- **FTC Final Rule on Fake Reviews (August 2024)** — explicitly prohibits AI-generated fake reviews and testimonials; penalties up to $51,744 per violation
- **Wis. Stat. 100.18** — prohibits untrue, deceptive, or misleading representations in advertising

## What CWDB Cannot Do
1. Display AI-generated testimonials as if from real customers — even with a "results may vary" disclaimer
2. Use disclaimers to cure fake testimonials — no disclaimer language makes fabricated endorsements lawful
3. Attribute fake quotes to named or unnamed "customers"
4. Use stock photos with fabricated quotes

## What CWDB Can Do Instead
1. **Remove all fake testimonials immediately** (before go-live)
2. Replace with factual, non-testimonial trust signals: years of experience (of contractor partners), number of projects completed, service area coverage, license/insurance verification badges
3. After real customers exist: collect genuine testimonials with written consent (keep consent records)
4. Use "What Our Process Looks Like" or "How It Works" sections instead of fake social proof
5. Display contractor credentials/certifications (with contractor permission)

## Resolution Status
- **OPEN** — AI testimonials remain on staging site as of 2026-04-04
- **Action required:** Remove before site goes live
- **Severity:** This is a GO-LIVE BLOCKER alongside privacy policy and TCPA consent language
